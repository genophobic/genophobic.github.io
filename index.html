About

Disheveled is an adult video game about a seasoned adventurer who, after a decade of adventuring, finally makes it big — only to lose everything. Left alone, in debt and stuck with an empty guild hall, he decides to start over on his own terms. If he's going to risk his life adventuring again, he’ll at least do it surrounded by hot babes.
